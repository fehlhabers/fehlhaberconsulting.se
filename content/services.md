We are two senior profiles with distinctly different strengths — and a shared conviction that the best outcomes come from combining them. Here's how we work, what we each bring, and what makes us more than the sum of our individual assignments.

## Two profiles, two lenses

**Katharina** brings the organisational lens. She navigates complexity — multiple stakeholders, parallel initiatives, competing priorities — and creates the structure, alignment and momentum needed to move forward. Product strategy, agile transformation, backlog and prioritisation, process design, leadership coaching. Her presence makes the difficult feel manageable and leaves teams feeling safe, clear and capable.

**Kaj** brings the technical lens. He architects platforms, builds AI-enabled workflows, designs cloud-native systems and creates the engineering culture that makes teams excel. Internal developer platforms, Kubernetes, CI/CD, GitOps, ensemble programming, AI agents. He doesn't just build — he embeds, experiments and shares what works.

## The multiplier effect

The real advantage isn't that you get one or the other. It's that even when you're working with one of us, you're getting both perspectives.

Technical architecture benefits from organisational thinking. Process design gets informed by what's actually possible to build. An AI adoption strategy gets pressure-tested against how change actually happens in organisations. A backlog gets healthier because the right technical questions were asked.

This isn't a sales pitch — it's a structural advantage. Two senior profiles, two domains of expertise, cross-pollinating across every assignment. The result is always stronger than what either could deliver alone.

## Ways to work with us

We take ownership and build trust — never distant advisors, always hands-on at the level where the work happens. Where that level sits depends on the profile.

- **Embedded in engineering teams** — full-time or part-time, as technical lead, architect, platform engineer, or an extra pair of senior hands. The most common format for Kaj, who works shoulder-to-shoulder with developers.
- **In leadership teams & management** — Product Manager, Agile Coach, or part of a steering group driving transformation across multiple teams and stakeholders. The natural habitat for Katharina, where structure, alignment and momentum are made.
- **Workshops & dojos** — focused sessions on AI-enabled development, ensemble programming, CI/CD, TDD, or remote collaboration practices.

Assignments range from short intensives to multi-year engagements. We work remotely with professional A/V setups, or physically in the Gothenburg area.
